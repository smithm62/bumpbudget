Paternity Leave (Ireland)

2 Weeks entitlement
€289/wk Paternity Benefit
26w Must take within

Your partner is entitled to 2 weeks paid Paternity Benefit from the Dept. of Social Protection, provided they have enough PRSI contributions. Must be taken within 26 weeks of the birth.

Parent's Leave (Ireland)

9 Weeks each parent
€289/wk Parent's Benefit
2yr Must take within

Both parents can take 9 weeks of Parent's Leave each, paid at €289/week. Must be taken within the first 2 years of the child's birth or adoption.

Help & Support

Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about BumpBudget.

?

Is BumpBudget free?

Yes. This version is built as an academic project and is free to use.

?

How does forecasting work?

Forecasting is based on your profile details - due date, income, and leave dates - alongside typical cost patterns from CSO Ireland data. Predictions are guidance, not financial advice.

?

Can I change my details later?

Yes. You can update your profile at any time from the sidebar to refresh your forecasts and plan.

?

Is my data private?

Your data is stored securely and is only visible to you. We do not share your information with third parties. As this is an academic project, we recommend not entering sensitive personal information.

?

How is my income decile calculated?

We use CSO Ireland SIA205 2025 household income data to place your income within the distribution of Irish earners. This helps us tailor your cost plan to what's realistic for your situation.

?

What is the maternity leave tracker?

The maternity leave tracker helps you map out your leave phases - full pay, half pay, statutory, and unpaid - and shows you how your income will change month by month so you can plan ahead with confidence.

?

What are savings goals?

Savings goals let you set targets for specific purchases or funds - like a pram, a hospital bag, or an emergency buffer. You can log contributions over time and track your progress toward each goal.

?

Can I use BumpBudget if I'm planning a pregnancy?

Absolutely. BumpBudget has a planning stage designed for people who are preparing for pregnancy. You can set up a budget, explore cost plans, and start saving goals before you're expecting.

?

Are the cost estimates accurate?

Cost estimates are based on real Irish household expenditure data and are tailored to your income level. They are intended as realistic guidance rather than exact figures - every family's situation is different.

?

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from your profile settings. This will permanently remove all your data including expenses, goals, and leave details. This action cannot be undone.
